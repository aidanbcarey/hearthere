Hear, There and Everywhere

Welcome! On this website you will see four pages (with the exception of this one).

The first is the on login page, which urges you to connect your Spotify account.

Next, we have the querying page. This tells the workers to fetch the lyrics of your top n songs on Spotify (n specified by you - so interactive!)

Finally, the data pages! These compare the frequencies of words in the songs you listen to to those found in a reference dataset (all Billboard top songs of the 2010s).

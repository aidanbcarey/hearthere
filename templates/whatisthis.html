Hear, There and Everywhere

Welcome! On this website you will see four pages (with the exception of this one).

The first is the on login page, which urges you to connect your Spotify account.

Next, we have the querying page. This tells the workers to fetch the lyrics of your top n songs on Spotify (n specified by you - so interactive!). It usually takes a bit of time to get all the lyrics from Genius, so just wait around. When your data is ready, it will be available for viewing in the respective page. f you make a query after already having made a different quesry, then once your second query is done it will replace the current data on the viewing pages.

Finally, the data pages! These compare the frequencies of words in the songs you listen to to those found in a reference dataset (all Billboard top songs of the 2010s).
